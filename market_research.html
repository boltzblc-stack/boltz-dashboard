Market Research

Types of Market Research

Market research can be classified into various types, including:

  • Primary Research
  • Secondary Research
  • Qualitative Research
  • Quantitative Research

Data Collection Instruments

Common instruments for data collection include:

  • Surveys
  • Interviews
  • Focus Groups
  • Observations

Tabulation

Tabulation is the process of summarizing data in a table format, facilitating easier analysis.

Charts and Visualizations

Charts are a vital part of market research analysis. Types include:

  • Bar Charts
  • Pie Charts
  • Line Graphs

Reports

Market research reports present findings, interpretations, and recommendations.

Analysis

Analysis involves interpreting the collected data to draw conclusions and inform decision-making.
